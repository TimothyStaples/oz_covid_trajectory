Australian State COVID-19 trends (Mobile version)
Caseloads to 31st March (inclusive)

Left panel: Y-axis scores are total confirmed cases by date, based on the first date each state recorded 10 confirmed cases. Hover over points to see an estimated trajectory for the next week, based on the average increase over the past week, as well as daily percentage increase and time for cases to double. These are NOT epidemiology models, they are simple projections based on past trends.

Right panel: Y-axis on this panel are the daily increases based on a running 7-day trend. These are equivalent to the slopes of the trajectories shown when hovering over left panel points, minus one. Positive percentages indicate cases are growing, with a zero indicating stable case numbers.

Data sourced from John Hopkins University CCSE. Trajectory models are generated in R. All code is under an MIT License. Code is poorly formatted and scarily inefficient - use at your own risk!
